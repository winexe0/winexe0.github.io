Our Founders

Aryan Chandna

Aryan Chandna is a passionate computer enthusiast who started EcoComputers with the vision of promoting sustainable computing solutions. He is from San Jose, California and supports the Right to Repair bill with the aim of making sure that people can repair their devices freely without any restrictions from the manufacturer.

Pratyush Srivastava

Pratyush Srivastava is a passionate Computer Programmer who attends Redwood Highschool Larkspur, CA. He founded EcoComputers alongside Aryan and Rohan, he works towards supporting the right to repair bill and expanding awareness on full cost pricing in envirnonmentalist society.
